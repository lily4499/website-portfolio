Monitoring (Prometheus + Grafana)

Observability setup for dashboards and alerting based on Golden Signals.

Project Summary

  • ProblemWithout monitoring, outages are detected late and root cause takes longer.
  • SolutionScrape metrics with Prometheus, visualize with Grafana, alert on latency/errors/saturation.
  • ToolsPrometheus · Grafana · Alert rules · Node/Kube metrics
  • OutcomeFaster incident detection, clear dashboards, and better understanding of system health trends.

Centralized Logging (ELK/EFK or Loki)

Central logging stack to search and analyze logs during incidents.

GitHub Project All Projects

Project Summary

  • ProblemLogs spread across nodes/containers make debugging slow and incomplete.
  • SolutionShip logs to a central system (ELK/EFK or Loki) for search, dashboards, and retention control.
  • ToolsElasticsearch · Logstash/Fluent Bit · Kibana OR Loki · Grafana
  • OutcomeFaster troubleshooting, easier correlation across services, and improved audit trails.
